# Board-Recordable Governance Framework

## I. Purpose

This document establishes the official board-recordable framework for the Goldston Family Office Blackbook and any related GFO token, digital asset, DAO discussion, educational initiative, publishing project, or community-governance research initiative.

The framework exists to preserve a clear written record that GFO is designed as a non-custodial, service-oriented, educational, publishing, governance, identity, and research ecosystem.

GFO is not designed to operate as a bank, broker-dealer, exchange, investment adviser, trust company, commodity pool, money transmitter, custodial wallet provider, securities issuer, or public asset manager.

## II. Legal Position

GFO is not designed to custody, manage, pool, transmit, invest, trade, safeguard, redeem, or administer assets on behalf of the public.

GFO is designed as a family-office-aligned research, publishing, education, governance, identity, and community-coordination framework.

Any GFO token, if deployed, must be structured and described as a non-custodial ecosystem participation instrument.

It must not be represented as:

- An investment product
- A profit-sharing instrument
- An equity interest
- A debt instrument
- A pooled fund interest
- A deposit product
- A revenue-share right
- A redemption claim
- A managed account
- A claim on family-office assets

## III. Board Resolution

**Resolved**, that the Goldston Family Office Blackbook and any related GFO token initiative shall be developed, described, and administered as a non-custodial, education-oriented, research-oriented, publishing-oriented, identity-oriented, and community-coordination framework.

**Resolved**, that GFO shall not accept, hold, pool, manage, safeguard, trade, transmit, redeem, or custody public assets.

**Resolved**, that GFO shall not market any token as an investment contract, security, equity interest, debt instrument, revenue-share right, deposit product, custodial claim, redemption claim, or managed financial product.

**Resolved**, that all public-facing GFO materials shall include appropriate disclaimers stating that GFO does not provide legal, tax, financial, investment, custody, brokerage, banking, exchange, money-transmission, or fiduciary services.

**Resolved**, that any future activity involving custody, payment transmission, exchange services, public investment management, pooled assets, grants funded by token activity, token redemption, or treasury-linked economic benefits shall require prior written legal review before implementation.

**Resolved**, that the official GFO record shall preserve copies of this framework, all public disclosures, all board approvals, all counsel comments, all material revisions, and all risk-control documents.

## IV. Governing Rule

**GFO may coordinate a thesis, publish research, support education, and enable non-custodial participation. GFO must not act as custodian for the public.**
