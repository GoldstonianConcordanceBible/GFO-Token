# GFO Non-Custody Policy

## I. Core Rule

GFO does not custody public assets.

GFO shall not accept deposits, hold customer funds, hold customer digital assets, manage user wallets, control user private keys, maintain hosted wallets, execute trades for users, pool user assets, or promise safekeeping of user property.

All users, participants, readers, token holders, and community members remain responsible for their own wallets, private keys, transfers, tax reporting, compliance obligations, trading decisions, custody decisions, and risk exposure.

## II. Prohibited Custody Language

No GFO representative may state, imply, advertise, publish, message, or suggest that GFO will:

- Hold user assets
- Protect user assets
- Safeguard user assets
- Manage user assets
- Trade user assets
- Guarantee user assets
- Redeem user assets
- Custody user assets
- Pool user assets
- Transmit user assets
- Operate hosted wallets
- Recover private keys
- Control user wallets

## III. Required Custody Boundary

GFO shall not provide custody services.

If any proposed activity appears to require regulated custody, GFO shall seek qualified legal counsel prior to proceeding and shall not activate that activity unless a lawful structure is approved in writing.

GFO shall not receive assets from the public for safekeeping, management, redemption, yield, pooling, trading, or transfer.

## IV. Public Disclosure Language

Approved language:

**GFO does not custody user funds or digital assets. Participants are solely responsible for their own wallets, private keys, transfers, transactions, tax reporting, compliance obligations, and risk decisions.**

Approved short form:

**Self-custody by the holder. No public custody by GFO.**

## V. Escalation Rule

Any proposed feature, partnership, bot, wallet, bridge, swap interface, book-redemption mechanic, DAO treasury mechanic, or token-holder benefit that could involve asset custody, asset pooling, managed execution, redemption, or transmission must be paused for legal review before public launch.

## VI. Final Rule

**GFO coordinates the experiment. GFO does not custody the public.**
