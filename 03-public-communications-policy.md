# Public Communications Policy

## I. Purpose

This policy governs public statements, book descriptions, GitHub documentation, website copy, social posts, livestreams, community messages, DAO posts, token descriptions, and educational materials related to the Goldston Family Office Blackbook, GFO token, and related community-governance research initiatives.

The purpose is to ensure that all public statements remain consistent with the non-custodial, no-investment-promise, no-redemption-guarantee, and no-public-asset-management posture.

## II. Required Anchor Phrase

The following phrase should appear consistently in core public materials:

**GFO coordinates the experiment. GFO does not custody the public.**

## V. Standard Response to Custody Questions

If anyone asks whether GFO can hold, manage, trade, safeguard, or custody assets, use:

**GFO does not custody public assets. Participants remain responsible for their own wallets, private keys, transfers, tax reporting, and risk decisions. Any activity that appears to require custody or asset transmission must be paused for legal review before proceeding.**

## VI. Standard Response to Investment Questions

If anyone asks whether GFO is an investment, use:

**GFO materials are for educational, publishing, governance, and research purposes only. GFO does not promise profit, yield, appreciation, liquidity, redemption, dividends, revenue share, or managerial efforts to increase token value.**

## VII. Standard Response to Compliance Questions

If anyone asks whether GFO is fully compliant, use:

**GFO is being structured as a compliance-aware, non-custodial framework subject to attorney review. No public-facing statement should be treated as a legal opinion or final regulatory determination.**

## VIII. Final Rule

**GFO coordinates the experiment. GFO does not custody the public.**
