## 1. Purpose

This ledger documents life events, contracts, publications, family-office activity, education work, digital asset activity, public communications, and court-ready evidence in a structured format.

GFO uses the phrase “tokenized life” to mean:

A transparent record of lived events, work product, contracts, publications, digital assets, family legacy, and governance actions organized for stewardship, provenance, accountability, investor diligence, and court-ready recordkeeping.

This ledger does not replace legal filings, estate documents, tax records, court orders, contracts, or accounting ledgers.
