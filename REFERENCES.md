# GFO Token Legal Framework References

## I. Purpose

This file records conceptual, research, interpretive, and technical references used to support the Goldston Family Office Token Legal Framework.

References listed in this file are not legal authorities unless expressly identified as statutes, regulations, court decisions, agency guidance, or signed legal opinions from qualified counsel.

This file exists to distinguish between:

- Binding legal authority
- Agency guidance
- Attorney-reviewed legal analysis
- Board records
- Non-binding research references
- Agent-readable legal taxonomy
- Decentralized governance research artifacts

## II. Core Reference

### Agent’s Law Dictionary: On-Chain AI Edition

**Reference Type:** Non-binding decentralized research initiative  
**Legal Status:** Not legally binding  
**Use Case:** Agent-readable legal vocabulary, smart-contract interpretation support, DAO governance terminology, decentralized jurisprudence research, on-chain compliance taxonomy, and autonomous-agent legal reasoning.

## III. Reference Description

*Agent’s Law Dictionary: On-Chain AI Edition* is an AI-optimized legal research resource designed for autonomous agents operating in decentralized, on-chain environments.

Inspired by traditional legal dictionaries, the work reframes legal concepts for blockchain jurisprudence, smart-contract logic, AI governance, decentralized finance, DAO operations, on-chain arbitration, and decentralized dispute-resolution systems.

Within the GFO Token Legal Framework, this reference is used only as a conceptual and interpretive aid for agent-readable legal architecture.

It does not replace:

- Statutes
- Regulations
- Court decisions
- Agency guidance
- Attorney review
- Board resolutions
- Jurisdiction-specific legal analysis
- Written legal opinions from qualified counsel
