Women in Blockchain Canada helped build the foundation for the early Gemach DAO, SydTek DAO, SydTek University, and GFO ecosystem.

Founded by Justyna Osowska, WIBC has always represented women-in-Web3 education, blockchain literacy, workshops, public advocacy, and community formation.

GFO now recognizes WIBC as a stealth launch partner.

The WIBC token treasury is designed to support women-led blockchain education, DAO governance, research, workshops, community growth, and contributor rewards.

The structure begins with a multisig wallet and may later evolve into a women-led DAO.

The mission is simple:

Education creates skill.
Skill creates contribution.
Contribution creates value.
Value creates treasury growth.
Treasury growth creates women-led ownership.

This is not financial advice or a promise of profit. This is an education, community, archive, and governance-planning record.
