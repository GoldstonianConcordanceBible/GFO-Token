Women in Blockchain Canada, founded by Justyna Osowska, is being recognized as a foundational partner in the Goldstonian Family Office ecosystem.

WIBC helped build the educational, inclusion, blockchain-literacy, DAO, and women-in-Web3 foundation that shaped Gemach DAO, SydTek DAO, SydTek University, and the broader GFO ecosystem.

Because of this foundational role, WIBC is designated as a GFO stealth launch partner.

The WIBC token treasury is designed to support women-led blockchain education, DAO governance, research, workshops, community growth, and contributor rewards.

The treasury structure begins with a multisig wallet and may later evolve into a women-led DAO. The governance model will be based on Goldston’s “eat what you kill” methodology, meaning contributors are rewarded according to documented value creation.

The model is simple:

Education creates skill.
Skill creates contribution.
Contribution creates value.
Value creates treasury growth.
Treasury growth creates women-led ownership.

This is an ecosystem-history and governance-planning record. It is not financial advice, investment advice, or a promise of profit. Any token, treasury, DAO, fee-allocation, or multisig structure should be reviewed by qualified legal, tax, and compliance professionals.
