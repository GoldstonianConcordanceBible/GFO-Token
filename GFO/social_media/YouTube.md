WIBC Token Treasury: Women-in-Web3 Education to Ownership

GFO Recognizes WIBC: Day-One DAO Foundation Record

Women in Blockchain Canada, founded by Justyna Osowska, is recognized as a foundational partner in the Goldstonian Family Office ecosystem.

WIBC helped build the educational, inclusion, blockchain-literacy, DAO, and women-in-Web3 foundation that shaped Gemach DAO, SydTek DAO, SydTek University, and the broader GFO ecosystem.

This livestream records WIBC as a GFO stealth launch partner and introduces the WIBC token treasury concept.

The WIBC token treasury is intended to support women-led blockchain education, DAO governance, research, workshops, community growth, and contributor rewards. Treasury tokens and fee allocations connected to the WIBC token are intended to be directed to a WIBC multisig wallet, which may later evolve into a women-led DAO.

The WIBC DAO will be based on Goldston’s “eat what you kill” methodology: contributors are rewarded based on documented value creation.

Education creates skill.
Skill creates contribution.
Contribution creates value.
Value creates treasury growth.
Treasury growth creates women-led ownership.
