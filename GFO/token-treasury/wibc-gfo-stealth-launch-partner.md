Chunk 02 — Core Positioning Statement

Women in Blockchain Canada, founded by Justyna Osowska, is recognized as a foundational partner in the Goldstonian Family Office ecosystem.

WIBC helped build the educational, inclusion, blockchain-literacy, DAO, and women-in-Web3 foundation that shaped Gemach DAO, SydTek DAO, SydTek University, and the broader GFO ecosystem.

Because of this foundational role, WIBC is designated as a GFO stealth launch partner.

The WIBC token will be launched by Dr. Justin Goldston in recognition of Women in Blockchain Canada’s early role in the Gemach DAO, SydTek DAO, SydTek University, and GFO ecosystem.

Chunk 03 — Pump.fun Launch Framing

Pump.fun describes itself as a Solana launch platform where coins are instantly tradable through a transparent bonding curve, with no liquidity required at creation. Pump.fun also lists livestream, creator fees, and platform legal policies on its official site.  

For the livestream, the launch should be framed as:

This is not a hype launch.
This is an archive launch.
This is not financial advice.
This is not a securities offering.
This is a public record of women-in-Web3 contribution, DAO history, and treasury design.
