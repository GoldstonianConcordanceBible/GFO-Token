## 1. Purpose

This index organizes GFO-related diligence materials for potential investors, strategic partners, advisors, attorneys, accountants, and governance reviewers.

## 2. Business Overview

Public summary of GFO:

GFO is a family-office documentation, publishing, education, consulting, governance, and digital asset transparency framework designed to preserve records, organize contracts, support investor diligence, and maintain non-custodial public accountability.
